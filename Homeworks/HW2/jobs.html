Work at JavaJam

Barista

As a Barista, you will be responsible for crafting exceptional coffee and providing excellent customer service in a fast-paced cafe environment. Your attention to detail, friendly demeanor, and passion for coffee will contribute to creating a welcoming and memorable experience for our customers. Your compensation will be $15.00 an hour

  • Prepare and serve a variety of coffee and espresso beverages with precision and consistency.
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace, including coffee machines, grinders, and serving areas.
  • Take customer orders, provide menu recommendations, and process payments accurately.

Cashier

As a Cashier, you will play a crucial role in providing excellent customer service and ensuring accurate financial transactions. Your friendly demeanor, attention to detail, and strong organizational skills will contribute to a positive shopping experience for our customers. Your compensation will be $10.00 an hour

  • Greet customers warmly as they enter the store and thank them for their business upon checkout.
  • Accurately and efficiently ring up customer purchases using a cash register or point-of-sale (POS) system.
  • Scan, weigh, or tag merchandise and ensure price accuracy.
